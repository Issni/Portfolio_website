hello!

My name is Airi Pan and I am a concept designer, illustrator, and creative director for games, animation, film, and more. To me, concept design, or illustration, isn’t merely about creating artwork. It's about understanding the final product, and how artwork helps serve something bigger than the design itself. How to deliver emotion. My philosophy mirrors my roots: an East meets West aesthetic that takes my training in Western art academia and blends it with my own culture and passion for Eastern pop culture from anime to fashion, to graphic design.



Currently, I am working as a Concept Artist at Riot Games
