Welcome to the Moon

You are a God coming to the Moon. Everyone comes to the moon to forget something,
what have you come to forget?

This Project is still WIP and is ongoing

The Stewards | BaiYu The moon is a spa for the gods— A place where the divine come to forget their memories Each arrives with a particular memory they no longer wish to carry, for various reasons good or bad. And now, you’ve come too, with your own memory to leave behind. The stewards are those who greet you as soon as you land on the island

The Gardeners | BaiYu The moon is a spa for the gods— A place where the divine come to forget their memories Each arrives with a particular memory they no longer wish to carry, for various reasons good or bad. And now, you’ve come too, with your own memory to leave behind. The plants here grow at horrendously fast speeds, and thrive off of memories, the only source of liquid. Thus, they can pose quite a problem to guests. The Gardeners are here to toil endlessly to keep them at bay everyday, the perfect job for endless toils
